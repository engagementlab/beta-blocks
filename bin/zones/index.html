Exploration Zones

Beta Blocks established a four square block area of relaxed permitting in each of three Boston neighborhoods. These "Exploration Zones" hosted temporary technology installations to facilitate community feedback to participating companies and the city.
Chinatown Codman Square, Dorchester Lower Allston

Chinatown

Located along the southern edge of Downtown, Boston's Chinatown is a commercial and cultural hub for the City's Chinese community.

Codman Square, Dorchester

Historic Codman Square is a major civic center in Dorchester, and is now one of the large neighborhood's major commercial hubs.

Lower Allston

Allston is close to many colleges and universities in and around the City, and recently many immigrants and young professionals have also moved to the area.
