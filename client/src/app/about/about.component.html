About Beta Blocks

Beta Blocks is an innovative strategy for supporting local Boston communities in pondering, questioning, and testing smart technology in exploration zones. Community members, in collaboration with technology providers and the City, set up real life experiments in the public realm and evaluate the impact of technologies on neighborhoods. This incorporates community values into the municipal technology procurement process from the very beginning.
