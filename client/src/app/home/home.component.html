Beta Blocks helps Boston think about whether and how technologies appear in the public realm.

To learn more, visit an interactive exhibit tour stop or join the Tech Explorers!

About

Beta blocks is a collaboration between the City of Boston Mayor's Office of New Urban Mechanics, the Engagement Lab @ Emerson College, and Supernormal.
