Becoming a Tech Explorer

Communities know their blocks inside out. Becoming a Tech Explorer gives you the chance to try smart tech before it hits the streets and share your ideas for how it could work for Boston.
This is your chance to turn your ideas for using and improving tech into real experiments. As at Tech Explorer, you’ll also learn lots of new things and have the chance to shape the future of Boston’s streets.

We are looking for Tech Explorers from all walks of life. All we ask is that you’re 12+, live or work in Greater Boston and can take part in six hours of meet ups and workshops. Absolutely no technical experience necessary, and we’ll give you a $100 gift card for your time.
